# Pedigree – Name Finalization & Rationale
**Date:** April 19, 2026  
**Status:** Approved for use (internal vote pending final trademark check)  
**Decision:** Primary name = **Pedigree** | Short name / URL = `getpedigree.com` or `pedigree.ai`

## Why "Pedigree"?

### Core Meaning Alignment
- **Lineage + Quality Control**: "Pedigree" literally means a record of descent or lineage, especially of a purebred animal or person of distinguished ancestry. Perfect metaphor for agents that must inherit controlled, auditable entitlements from a verified human parent.
- **Enterprise-Grade Tone**: Sounds premium, trustworthy, and technical without being dystopian. Avoids "Shadow" (negative, compliance-triggering) or generic security words ("Guard", "Sentry", "Forge").
- **Category Ownership**: Instantly communicates the thesis: *Every agent has a Pedigree — a documented human parent, scope envelope, and lifecycle tied to the org chart.*
- **Brandability**: Short, memorable, easy to verb ("Pedigree your agents"), noun ("the Pedigree graph"), and adjective ("Pedigree-compliant agents").

### Comparison to Alternatives Considered

| Name              | Pros                              | Cons                                      | Why Rejected |
|-------------------|-----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|--------------|
| **Shadow Org**    | Original placeholder, descriptive | "Shadow" = hidden/rogue (CISO nightmare); already used for informal org charts | Strong negative connotation |
| **Lineage**       | Clean, modern, exact technical meaning | Too generic; many products use it | Lacks premium feel |
| **AgentLineage**  | Explicit                        | Clunky, long for branding                 | "Pedigree" is shorter & richer |
| **RootGraph**     | Emphasizes HRIS root              | Sounds like infrastructure tool           | Too technical for marketers |
| **OrgAnchor**     | Good metaphor (anchored to humans) | "Anchor" feels heavy/static               | "Pedigree" wins on elegance |
| **HumanGraph**    | Clear primitive                   | "Human" may limit AI-native perception    | Pedigree feels more sophisticated |
| **AgentRoot**     | Simple                            | "Root" has multiple meanings (Linux, etc.) | Ambiguous |
| **Pedigree**      | All of the above + premium        | Minor trademark risk (common word)        | **Chosen** |

- **Logo Concept**: Stylized family tree / org chart node with a clean "P" monogram or ascending arrow from human to agent. Colors: Deep navy + electric teal accent (trust + innovation). Avoid red/black (security theater).
- **Tagline Options**:
  - "Every Agent Has a Pedigree."
  - "The Org Chart for Your AI Workforce."
